Antal Orcsik
Software Engineer
I'm a polyglot coder. Writing programs is not just my profession, but my greatest hobby. I'm mostly experienced in JavaScript, PHP and Python, but I feel myself comfortable in, C++, Java and Scala too. Over the years I also used SML, Racket, Ruby, Objective-C, and some other languages.

I started web development in 2000 with simple HTML pages with CSS, learned client side and server side programming languages while attending university. From 2005 I worked 2 years on a real-estate site, then another 5 on a weather portal at the same company.

Nowadays I work at Prezi as a backend developer, and attending university again. In my free time I develop a movie premiere collection site: Mozipremierek.hu

I live, work and play in the beautiful city of Budapest, Hungary.
